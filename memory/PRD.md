# Big Wheels Car Rentals - Landing Page PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a high-converting, modern, mobile-first landing page for a self-drive car rental company in Goa, India.

## User Persona
- **Primary:** Tourists visiting Goa from paid ads traffic
- **Goal:** Quick car rental booking via WhatsApp/Call

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Production-ready static website (HTML/CSS/JS)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- White background with modern aesthetics
- WhatsApp: 7020776990 | Phone: +917020776990
- 13 cars sorted low to high (₹1000 - ₹3500/day)
- "Minimum 2 Days Booking" on every car

## What's Been Implemented (Dec 2025)
- ✅ Hero section with CTAs and scenic background
- ✅ Trust badges (4): Minimum 2 Days, Instant Confirmation, Airport Delivery, Best Price
- ✅ 13 car listings with specs, pricing, WhatsApp prefilled messages
- ✅ Why Choose Us section (4 features with icons)
- ✅ Service Areas section (10 Goa locations)
- ✅ Testimonials (3 customer reviews)
- ✅ Final CTA section
- ✅ Sticky header with navigation
- ✅ Floating WhatsApp button
- ✅ Mobile responsive design
- ✅ Smooth scroll navigation
- ✅ Urgency banner

## Technical Stack
- Pure HTML5 + CSS3 + Vanilla JavaScript
- Google Fonts (Outfit + Plus Jakarta Sans)
- Font Awesome icons
- No framework dependencies
